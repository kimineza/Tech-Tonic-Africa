How To Make Panacakes

Pancakes are quick and easy breakfast option that everyone loves. Follow this simple guide to make fluffy and delicious pancakes at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 tea spoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon of soda
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 table spoons melted butter

    • Steps to Make Pancakes

    1.In a large bowl,mix the flour,sugar,baking powder,baking soda and salt.

    2.In another bowl,whisk together the milk,egg and melted butter

    3.Pour the wet ingridient into the dry ingridients and mix until just combined.Do not over mix

    4.Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease it.

    5.Pour 1/4 cup of butter onto the pan for each pancake

    6.Cook until bubbles from on the surface,then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.

    7.Serve warm with your favorite toppings like syrup,fruits,or whipped cream.
